# Portfolioer: Your Life as a Library

Portfolioer is a premium, 3D interactive bookshelf application designed to curate memories, achievements, and experiences as physical objects in a virtual library.

## ✨ Core Features

### 1. Immersive 3D Bookshelf
- **Warm Aesthetic**: A cozy library environment with peachy/beige tones and cream wooden shelves.
- **Dynamic Lighting**: Each shelf compartment features an overhead spotlight with realistic radial light beams and inner shadows.
- **Categorized Sections**: 20 distinct categories distributed across 6 rows, from Movies & Games to Skills and Memories.

### 2. Interactive Book Spines
- **Visual Variety**: Books vary in width and use a curated color palette specific to their category.
- **Realistic Textures**: Leather/fabric overlays and gold foil lettering give books a premium, tactile feel.
- **3D Hover Effects**: Hovering over a spine causes the book to tilt forward and glow, inviting interaction.

### 3. Immersive Detail View
- **Slide & Center**: Clicking a book smoothly transitions it from the shelf to the center of the screen.
- **3D Page Flipping**: An advanced page-flipping mechanism allows users to open books and flip through content with realistic `preserve-3d` animations.
- **Rich Content**: Support for images, ratings, metadata, and multi-page stories.

### 4. Smart Library Management
- **Search & Filter**: Instantly find books across all shelves with a global search bar.
- **Edit Mode**: A dedicated toggle for owners to manage their library, featuring placeholder slots and visual feedback.

## 🛠️ Technical Stack
- **Framework**: Next.js 15 (App Router)
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS v4
- **Animations**: Framer Motion (3D transforms, Layout animations)
- **Icons**: Heroicons & Lucide
- **Typography**: Google Fonts (Playfair Display for titles, Outfit for sans-serif)

## 📖 How to Use
1. **Explore**: Scroll through the bookshelf to see different sections of the curator's life.
2. **Hover**: Interact with book spines to see their titles and details.
3. **Open**: Click a book to bring it to focus, then click again to open and flip through its pages.
4. **Search**: Use the search bar to filter specific items or categories.
5. **Manage**: Toggle "Edit Mode" to see how the library can be expanded.
